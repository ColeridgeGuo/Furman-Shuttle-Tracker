The Daytime Shuttle: Monday - Friday, 7:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m., and 1:00p.m. to 4:00p.m. on a fixed-route. 
Pick-up and drop-off locations include North Village J, the Dining Hall, the Library, and the PAC. 
 
The Evening SafeRide: 7 days a week, from 7:00 p.m. to 1:00 a.m. 
TEL: 864-772-0007. 
If the SafeRide Shuttle is unavailable students may always request a Police escort on campus by calling: 864-294-2111.
 
The Downtown Trolley: Friday and Saturday nights from 10:00 p.m. to 2:00 a.m. 
Pick-up and drop-off locations include North Village J, the Vinings, and the intersection of Main and Coffee Streets downtown. 
This will be available throughout the semester, with the exceptions of October 5th-6th, November 23rd-24th, and December 14th-15th.
 
The Walmart Shuttle: Tuesdays, 6:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m. 
The pick-up is at Judson Hall. This shuttle will be available throughout the semester through December 11th.
  
As a reminder, a valid Furman ID is required when using all Furman University Police Department and SGA transportation services.
